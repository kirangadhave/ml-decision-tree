\section{Linear Classifier}
\label{sec:q2}

\item The given table can be represented by the following linear classifier:\\
$$

            1  & 1  & 1  & 0  & 1				& 1  \\

\item


The classifer, \\
$$x_1 + x_4 \geq 1$$

\end{enumerate}
